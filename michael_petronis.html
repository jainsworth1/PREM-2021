Research Description

Magnetostraigraphy, rock magnetism, paleomagnetism, and ground magnetometry, structural geology, tectonics, and geologic mapping from the high-Arctic of Svalbard, Norway, East Greenland, Scotland, and China, volcanic studies in the Chaine des Puys in France, Czech Republic, Idaho, New Mexico, and western Nevada, and paleoclimate studies in the western and central USA.
